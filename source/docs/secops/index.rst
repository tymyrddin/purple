Integrated security operations
============================================

.. image:: /_static/images/secops.png
  :alt: A clean, minimalist illustration of a simplest stick person standing between two simple icons: a shield representing security operations and a lightning bolt representing incident response. The stick person is connecting the two with a confident stance, as if bridging the gap.

Integrated security operations is where detection meets action without the usual bureaucratic detour. It brings SOC
vigilance and SIRT decisiveness into one continuous loop, focused on catching trouble early, responding fast, and
learning even faster.
