Roleplays
===============================================

A roleplay puts people into a situation and asks them to act it out until the acting stops and the real
communication patterns show through: who placates, who blames, who retreats into procedure, who is willing
to say the uncomfortable thing. The evidence is behavioural. It surfaces the distance between the policy on
the page and what people actually do when the pressure is on.
