# Purple session sequence

A four-session arc for working through purple team practice as collaborative sessions rather than as documentation. The sequence is editorial: each session feeds the next.

1. [Scenario labs](../workshops/scenario-labs.md), explore plausible situations and experiment with responses.
2. [Live experiments](../workshops/live-experiments.md), test team responses with controlled, real-time challenges.
3. [Rapid retrospectives](../workshops/rapid-retrospectives.md), capture lessons immediately, before they fade.
4. [Collaborative playbooks](../workshops/collaborative-playbooks.md), turn lessons into practical guidance.

Working through the four in order produces a shared understanding of how scenarios might unfold, hands-on experience responding to simulated challenges, and practical playbooks the team has built rather than been handed.

The sessions are designed to be iterative and adaptable. Repeat, tweak, and learn continuously.
