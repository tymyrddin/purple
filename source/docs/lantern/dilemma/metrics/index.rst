Metrics of stability
=============================================================

The invisible forces that determine a ruler's fate: Public Trust, Budget, Regulatory Pressure, Political Stability,
and Legitimacy. These are the vital signs of the polity itself, the measures by which the Patrician knows if his city
is merely grumbling or on the verge of consuming itself.
