From playbooks to simulator scenarios
=========================================

When `The Scarlet Semaphore <https://red.tymyrddin.dev/docs/scarlet/op-red-lantern/>`_ executes an operation, they
know exactly what they're doing and why, or at least they think they do. They do have good visibility into their own
actions. They understand their intent, their timing, their tooling. They know which actions are deliberate and
which are accidents. They know when they're succeeding and when they're improvising.

Defenders have none of this.

Defenders see logs. They see alerts. They see traffic patterns. They see state changes in systems. They don't see
intent. They don't know whether what they're observing is attack, misconfiguration, automation failure,
