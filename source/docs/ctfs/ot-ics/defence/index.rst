European OT Defence Reference
===============================================

Attack technique coverage without a defensive counterpart is half the picture. These pages map European
OT threat likelihood and detection feasibility so that challenge designers and practitioners can calibrate
what realistic defensive scenarios look like.

The threat landscape page frames each technique by likelihood and impact in the European context. The
detection priorities page maps what can realistically be detected, by what method, and where often the gaps are.
