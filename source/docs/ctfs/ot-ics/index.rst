OT/ICS CTF Forge
===============================================

Industrial control systems run the world's critical infrastructure: power grids, water treatment plants, manufacturing
lines, and pipelines. Yet security testing on live environments is dangerous, expensive, and often impossible. A
single misplaced packet can cause physical damage, production downtime, or safety incidents.

Well-designed challenges allow security practitioners to learn OT attack paths, protocol analysis, and operational
consequences without risking real equipment.

Root-Me is the lower-friction starting point: no VM to build, no upload pipeline, just assets and a flag. TryHackMe
is the more ambitious option, and the harder one to get right.
