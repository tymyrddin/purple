The auditor's toolkit
======================================================

These pages apply across all the framework-specific sections rather than belonging to any single one. Standards and
regulations name the obligations; attack taxonomy and the CIA triad build the conceptual vocabulary; gap analysis and
threat register are the working methods behind structured findings. The tooling pages cover BI and AI options for
evidence gathering and monitoring at scale.
