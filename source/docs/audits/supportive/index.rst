Auditing toolkit
======================================================

The toolkit is the reference layer that applies across all four frameworks: the concepts and
obligations, the working methods, the audit process itself, and the tooling. For a reader arriving from a
playbook stage, the map points at the tools that usually apply there.

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 40 60

   * - Arriving from
     - Worth opening
   * - Choosing and scoping a route (the map room, exploring the crossing, the floor walk, surveying the fortress)
     - :doc:`Scope definition <scope-definition>`, :doc:`ISO/IEC standards <standards>`, :doc:`OT/ICS standards <ot-standards>`, :doc:`EU regulations <eu-regulations>`
   * - Assessing risk and impact (the risk tent, understanding the river, storm charts, knowing the besiegers)
     - :doc:`Threat register <threat-register>`, :doc:`Risk scoring <risk-scoring>`, :doc:`Business impact analysis <business-impact-analysis>`, :doc:`Arrows and shields <arrows-shields>`, :doc:`Interview facilitation <interview-facilitation>`
   * - Building the controls (the gear depot, building a raft, the emergency systems, walls and gates, locks and patrols)
     - :doc:`Gap analysis <gap-analysis>`, :doc:`Supply chain risk <supply-chain>`, :doc:`CIA in practice <cia>`
   * - Operating and testing (the climb, into the current, running the drills, testing the defences)
     - :doc:`Evidence <evidence>`, :doc:`Continuous monitoring <continuous-monitoring>`, :doc:`Corrective action <corrective-action>`
   * - Facing the audit (base camp checks, the summit push, the far bank, the dossier, the inspection)
     - :doc:`Evidence <evidence>`, :doc:`Findings and reporting <findings-reporting>`, :doc:`Certification bodies <certification-body>`, :doc:`Interview facilitation <interview-facilitation>`
   * - Staying compliant (the flag, staying afloat, after the storm, keeping watch)
     - :doc:`Continuous monitoring <continuous-monitoring>`, :doc:`Corrective action <corrective-action>`, :doc:`AI tooling <ai-in-audits>`, :doc:`BI tooling <bi-in-audits>`
