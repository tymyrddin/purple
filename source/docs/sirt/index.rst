Building an Incident Response Team (SIRT)
============================================

.. image:: /_static/images/sirt.png
  :alt: Stick-figure team gathered around a table with laptops and papers, one figure pointing at a large stick-diagram of a cyber-attack flow, others taking notes.

A lean, agile SIRT focuses on essentials: clear roles, flexible processes, and continuous learning. It avoids heavy
bureaucracy while being ready for realistic incident scenarios.
